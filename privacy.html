Privacy Policy
Clarity Tools Australia
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Clarity Tools Australia (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to maintaining transparency and protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when users interact with our applications, websites, and related services, including our game Margin.

This document is intentionally detailed to provide full clarity regarding our practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information. Specifically:

• Name — When voluntarily submitted through contact forms.
• Analytics Data — Anonymous usage and performance data.
• Advertising Data — Limited information used to serve advertisements.

We do not collect sensitive health data, financial information, biometric data, government identification numbers, precise location data, or private messages beyond what is voluntarily submitted in forms.

2. Name Collection

When a user submits their name via a form, that name is used solely to respond to inquiries or provide support. We do not sell, rent, or share names with unrelated third parties for marketing purposes.

Names are stored securely and retained only as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill communication purposes.

3. Analytics Information

We collect anonymized analytics data to understand how our applications perform and how users interact with them. This may include:

• Device type
• Operating system
• App version
• Session duration
• Feature usage statistics
• Crash logs
• General geographic region (country-level only)

Analytics data does not directly identify individuals. It is aggregated and used solely to improve functionality, stability, and user experience.

4. Advertising

Our applications may display advertisements. Advertising partners may collect limited device-related information to provide relevant ads. This may include:

• Device identifiers
• General location data (not precise)
• Interaction data related to ads

We do not personally identify users for advertising purposes. Advertising data is handled in accordance with applicable privacy regulations.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

• Respond to user inquiries
• Improve application performance
• Diagnose technical issues
• Analyze trends
• Deliver advertisements
• Maintain security and prevent abuse

We do not use personal information for profiling unrelated to our services.

6. Legal Basis

Where applicable, we process data based on legitimate interest, user consent where required, and necessity to provide requested services.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share limited data with:

• Analytics providers (aggregated usage data only)
• Advertising networks (for ad delivery)
• Service providers assisting in app infrastructure

All partners are required to implement appropriate safeguards.

8. Data Retention

Names submitted through forms are retained only as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry.

Analytics and advertising data may be retained in aggregated form for performance monitoring and reporting purposes.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take appropriate steps to safeguard information.

10. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside Australia. Where this occurs, we ensure safeguards consistent with applicable laws.

11. User Rights

Depending on location, users may have rights to:

• Access their information
• Request correction
• Request deletion
• Withdraw consent for certain processing activities

Requests may be submitted via our contact form.

12. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date. Continued use of our services after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

14. Contact

Clarity Tools Australia
support@claritytoolsaustralia.com

This Privacy Policy is intended to provide comprehensive transparency regarding our data practices.
